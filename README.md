# Advanced Media Player

Advanced Media Player is a simple, fast, and accessible desktop media player. It allows you to play your favorite music and video files, adjust playback speed and pitch, customize your sound using a built-in equalizer, and stream or download content directly from YouTube.

This player is designed with a strong focus on accessibility. It integrates directly with the **NVDA screen reader** on Windows to announce status changes, volume adjustments, and track progress, making it easy to use for blind and visually impaired users.

---

## Key Features

* **Advanced Audio Controls**: Adjust the playback speed (tempo) and pitch independently, or adjust your sound frequencies using the built-in **10-Band Equalizer**.
* **Sound Card Selector**: Choose which speaker or audio device the player should output sound through. You can switch devices instantly during playback.
* **NVDA Screen Reader Support**: Announces current track progress, volume levels, play/pause alerts, and speed changes automatically.
* **YouTube Playback & Search**: Search for videos directly within the player and play them instantly.
* **YouTube Downloader**: Download YouTube videos as audio (MP3, M4A, WAV) or video (MP4) files to your computer.
* **Auto-Updates**: The player automatically checks for updates on startup to keep the application and YouTube streaming features working smoothly.
* **Default Player Registration**: Easily set Advanced Media Player as your default system player for all common audio and video files.

## Keyboard Shortcuts Guide

Use these keys to navigate and control the player quickly:

### Playback & Volume Controls
* **`Space`**: Pause or Resume media.
* **`Left Arrow` / `Right Arrow`**: Rewind or Fast-forward (default: 5 seconds).
* **`Up Arrow` / `Down Arrow`**: Turn the volume up or down.
* **`V`**: Speak the current volume level.
* **`T`**: Speak the current track progress (e.g. "1 minute 20 seconds of 3 minutes").
* **`Home`**: Restart the current track from the beginning.
* **`Tab` / `Page Down`**: Play the next track in the folder.
* **`Shift + Tab` / `Page Up`**: Play the previous track in the folder.

### Speed & Pitch Controls
* **`Ctrl + Up Arrow`**: Speed up playback by `0.1x` (up to `2.0x`).
* **`Ctrl + Down Arrow`**: Slow down playback by `0.1x` (down to `0.5x`).
* **`Alt + Up Arrow`**: Raise the sound pitch.
* **`Alt + Down Arrow`**: Lower the sound pitch.

### File & YouTube Search
* **`Ctrl + O`**: Open a dialog to choose and play a media file.
* **`Ctrl + F`**: Open a dialog to select and load an entire folder.
* **`S`**: Show the list of files in the currently playing folder (Searchable).
* **`Ctrl + S`**: Open the YouTube Search box.
* **`Escape`**: Return to YouTube search results list (when playing a video).

### Options & Updates
* **`Ctrl + Shift + O`**: Open the Settings/Options menu.
* **`Ctrl + Shift + E`**: Open the Equalizer to adjust sound frequencies.
* **`Ctrl + Shift + U`**: Manually check for program updates.
* **`Ctrl + Shift + A`**: Open the "About the Program" info dialog.
* **`Ctrl + Shift + C`**: Open the "Contact" details dialog.
* **`Ctrl + C`**: Copy the current file path or YouTube link to the clipboard.
* **`Escape`**: Close any active dialog window.
* **`Ctrl + E`** / **`Esc`**: Exit the program.

## Settings and Adjustments

### 1. Sound Device Selection
You can change the speaker or headphone output by going to **Options (Ctrl+Shift+O) > Audio Settings** and selecting your preferred device in the **Choose the audio output device (sound card)** dropdown.

### 2. Auto-Play & Repeat
Under **Options (Ctrl+Shift+O) > General Settings**, you can choose whether the player should automatically repeat the current track or automatically play the next track in the folder.

### 3. File Associations
If you want Advanced Media Player to open automatically when you double-click music or video files, open **Options (Ctrl+Shift+O) > General Settings** and click **Set as default media player**. This will register the player on your system and open the Windows default apps screen for confirmation.
